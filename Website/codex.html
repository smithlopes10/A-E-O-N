What is CP? 🤔

Competitive programming involves programmers competing against each other to solve programming questions in a limited amount of time.⏳🧑‍💻The results are judged on the number of questions solved, time spent solving the problems, and other factors, like execution time, program size, etc.🏆

Why should you do CP? 📑

▶️Makes you a desirable candidate to major companies
▶️Helps you solve complicated problems
▶️Makes a huge impact on your Resume

What is DSA? 🤔

DSA is Data structures and Algorithms and a person equipped with strong DSA skills will have better and more efficient approaches to solving a problem than someone who is not aware of DSA concepts.😎🔥

Why should you do DSA? 📑

▶️DSA helps in cracking product based company interviews
▶️Learning DSA helps in writing optimised code
▶️Efficiency is the main reason why interviews in top companies are focused on DSA as they want people to design algorithms that can save the company thousands of dollars.
